I'm passionate about innovation at the intersection of medical informatics, data discovery, computational pathology, AI/ML, and digital health. I transform complex biomedical datasets into actionable insights, whether by developing ML models for digital pathology or ensuring high-quality, reproducible data workflows that support scientific discovery and clinical decision-making.
With strong interdisciplinary foundations in biomedical data science and medical informatics, I manage large-scale imaging datasets, annotation-rich pipelines, and complex biomedical analyses, while ensuring data quality, compliance, and reproducibility. I thrive in cross-functional collaborations, translating complex scientific and technical requirements into structured, actionable datasets to enable AI-driven solutions that improve patient outcomes.
I am also driven by innovation and entrepreneurship, eager to explore new ideas, contribute to transformative solutions in digital health and computational pathology.
